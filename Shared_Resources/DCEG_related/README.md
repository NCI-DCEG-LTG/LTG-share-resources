#### DCGE course about genomic analysis and statistical workshop:

[DCEG Github workshop](https://dceg-workshops.github.io/fair_principles_for_data_stewardship/workshops/workshop_2)

- Introduction to GitHub Workshop

[FAIR Principles for Data Stewardship
](https://dceg-workshops.github.io/fair_principles_for_data_stewardship/)

- The series of workshops organized by The Trans-Divisional Research Program (TDRP) will cover topics such as how to manage, govern, and analyze data stored in the Cloud, use version control to track and annotate your data and programs, use GitHub to support collaborative research, programming practices to support reproducibility and replication, among other topics related to FAIR research.

[2022-2023 Emerging Approaches for Tumor Analyses in Epidemiological Studies](https://nci-iteb.github.io/tumor_epidemiology_approaches/)

- The series of courses organized by the Integrative Tumor Epidemiology Branch (ITEB), and taught by bioinformaticians from the Division of Cancer Epidemiology and Genetics (DCEG), NCI; the University of Chicago, and the University of California San Diego, many of whom are involved in the Sherlock-Lung study of lung cancer in never smokers.

[2023 DCEG Statistical Genetics Workshop](https://dceg-workshops.github.io/statistical_genetics_workshop/)

- This course will provide researchers and analysts with a review of cutting-edge statistical methods and hands-on tutorials for analyzing large-scale genome-wide genetic data. The Google Colab notebooks are available on the GitHub Repository.
